Understanding BMI

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI of 30 or more

Note: BMI is a general indicator and may not be accurate for all individuals, such as athletes or pregnant women. Always consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive health assessment.
